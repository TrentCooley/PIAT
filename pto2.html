Personal Summary

The gift of the holy ghost is a gift that is given to us By God. This gift can help us throughout our lives as long as we are worthy. There are many different ways that we might receive the Holy Ghost's help. This help might come to us at work at home and help us with many tasks. There are also gifts that the spirit can give to us to better help ourselves and others around us. There are many gifts of the spirit and we can gain these gifts by prayer and the use of talents that we already have.

Enduring to the end is one of the most important actions that we must do. There are many temptations in the world and it can be difficult to endure to the end. Enduring might seem that we are taking a passive stance to sin, but we must repent often for mistakes that we make in order to endure.

Bible Dictonary

Gift of the Holy Ghost

All honest seekers of the truth can feel the influence of the Holy Ghost, leading them to Jesus Christ and His gospel. However, the fulness of the blessings given through the Holy Ghost are available only to those who receive the gift of the Holy Ghost and remain worthy.

After a person is baptized into The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, one or more Melchizedek Priesthood holders lay their hands on the person’s head and, in a sacred priesthood ordinance, confirm him or her a member of the Church. As part of this ordinance, called confirmation, the person is given the gift of the Holy Ghost.

2 Nephi 31:18

And then are ye in this strait and narrow path which leads to eternal life; yea, ye have entered in by the gate; ye have done according to the commandments of the Father and the Son; and ye have received the Holy Ghost, which witnesses of the Father and the Son, unto the fulfilling of the promise which he hath made, that if ye entered in by the way ye should receive.

Explanation

This scripture talks about how the Holy Ghost is a witness of God and Christ. This witness comes after we have received the Holy Ghost.

Alma 9:21

Having been visited by the Spirit of God; having conversed with angels, and having been spoken unto by the voice of the Lord; and having the spirit of prophecy, and the spirit of revelation, and also many gifts, the gift of speaking with tongues, and the gift of preaching, and the gift of the Holy Ghost, and the gift of translation;

Explanation

This scripture talks about the many gifts of the spirit that we can gain after we receive the Holy Ghost.

Moroni 10:7-8

7 And ye may know that he is, by the power of the Holy Ghost; wherefore I would exhort you that ye deny not the power of God; for he worketh by power, according to the faith of the children of men, the same today and tomorrow, and forever.

8 And again, I exhort you, my brethren, that ye deny not the gifts of God, for they are many; and they come from the same God. And there are different ways that these gifts are administered; but it is the same God who worketh all in all; and they are given by the manifestations of the Spirit of God unto men, to profit them.

Explanation

In these scripture verses we can see the importance of the gifts that God has given us. These gifts can be gift of the Holy Ghost and the gifts of the spirit.
